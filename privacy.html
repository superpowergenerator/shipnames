Privacy Policy

Last updated July 16, 2026. Your privacy matters to us, so here is exactly how this tool works.

The short version: This generator creates ship names entirely inside your own browser. The names you type are never sent to our servers, because there is no server-side processing of them.

1. Information We Collect

This tool is designed to be privacy-friendly. The names you enter into the generator are processed locally in your browser and are not transmitted to, or stored by, us.

We may collect limited, non-identifying technical information through standard analytics (such as page views, browser type, and general region) to understand how the site is used and to improve it.

Contact form

If you use our contact form, you voluntarily provide your name, email address, and message so we can respond. We use this information solely to reply to your inquiry.

2. Cookies

We may use a small number of cookies or similar local-storage technologies to remember basic preferences and to power analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; the generator will continue to work without them.

3. Third-Party Services

We load web fonts from Google Fonts, which may process your IP address as part of serving those fonts. If we use an analytics provider, it may set its own cookies subject to its privacy policy. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Security

Because ship-name generation happens locally and we collect minimal data, your exposure is low by design. Any information you send us through the contact form is handled with reasonable care.

5. Children's Privacy

The Ship Name Generator is a general-audience entertainment tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information you have shared with us. Contact us to make such a request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out through our contact page.
