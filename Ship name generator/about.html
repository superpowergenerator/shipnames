About the Ship Name Generator

The little tool that turns two names into one blended ship name.

What is a ship name?

A ship name, short for "relationship name," is a single blended nickname created by combining two names. Fans of books, shows, and celebrity pairings have used them for years to talk about their favorite couples: mix the front of one name with the tail of another and you get something like Alex + Emma becoming Alemma.

The ship name generator takes that idea and makes it easy. Type two names, pick a vibe, and get ten pronounceable, ready-to-share blends in a single click.

Why do people use ship names?

Ship names are playful, personal, and easy to share. People reach for them when they want a nickname that feels like it belongs to two people at once.

  • Couples create a cute pet name for themselves to use on socials or in private.
  • Fiction writers and fans name pairings in their stories and communities.
  • Wedding planners spin up hashtags and monograms that combine both partners.
  • Creators land memorable usernames, handles, and team names.
  • Parents-to-be use blends as fresh baby-name inspiration.

How the generator works

Behind the scenes, the tool runs your two names through a set of blending strategies: head-of-one plus tail-of-the-other, syllable splices, short nickname styles, and more. It then smooths awkward letter clusters so every result stays easy to say, removes duplicates, and shapes the output to match the style you picked.

Everything runs right in your browser. No names are stored, and nothing is sent to a server, so your inputs never leave your device.
